FOREVERMORE

The Last One to Stand

Allow me to introduce you to Sceletus Adami, better known by the folks as Forevermore. You might ask, what is this? A hero? An anti-hero? A villain? To keep it short, none of those. Forevermore is a wandering entity with no purpose, no goal, no good or bad intentions, and nothing to achieve nor lose, only himself and his forever-lasting presence in the universe until everything comes to an inevitable end. Forevermore seems to be nothing but a skeleton, a walking, talking skeleton that travels from country to country aimlessly, simply wondering when will the seven trumpets be heard.

"I am both, peace and chaos. I'll be there for the beginning, and the end."

-Forevermore
