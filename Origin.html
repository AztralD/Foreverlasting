God's Ultimatum

A very long time ago, Forevermore was one of many kings reigning over one of the biggest civilizations back in time, going by name of "King Adamus". He was greedy for power, like all kings in that time, and as he desired more and more, everything led his way towards the dark magic, which he started learning, mastering and using for his own, selfish needs. You'd think, as any history like this one, someone would go against his power and end his reign of terror, but that's not case... Adamus reigned over 10 years, spreading chaos and fear with every step he took into different kingdoms. No one was able to stop him, his power was way too powerful for even the most experienced wizards to be able to stand against him, but that was until IT appeared... ███████, God of these lands, confronted the King, managing to end his life after severañ hours of fighting, however, death wasn't enough punishment for this monster, and so, ███████ sentenced Adamus to what he is now. A skinless, muscleless and organless entity with absolutely no purpose in life but to presence, live and repeat the end of time, for eternity.
You might think this is a horrible fate, and it is, but to be fair... This is what happens when you try to play God.

"I am both, peace and chaos. I'll be there for the beginning, and the end."

-Forevermore
