An Unstoppable Force

Curse #1 : "I always come back"
Forevermore can't psychically nor mentally die. His body and mind were made to endure the worst possible scenarios as the years pass by in his eternal life. His body, if damaged, will simply reconstruct all by itself no matter how severe the damage was, even if his skeletal body is completely crushed, from the smallest remains of his physical form and the ashes across the ground, it'll simply come back. His mind is also meant to take and bear all kinds of damnation, the worst traumas, the harshest decisions, everything that would drive a normal human crazy, but Forevermore is no human, not anymore.
Curse #2 : "Peace is an old friend"
The world ends, the universe collapses, everything fades to black, however, Forevermore will be there. The moment all life in the universe vanish, Forevermore will too, but his essence, and for that matter, his soul, will not and never be gone. Even if the universe is left as a dark abyss of complete darkness, his essence will lay there, living every single moment until the universe rebuilds itself for new eons of life to come, and so, Forevermore will experience every second of it.
Curse #3 : "It's been a while"
As previously stated, Forevermore's mind was made to endure everything and anything, and as for that, wisdom and knowledge fills his mind with every year, decade, century, millennium and eon. Forevermore can easily be considered the smartest entity in the planet for the constant gain of knowledge over the nonstop years due to his infinite existence.

"I am both, peace and chaos. I'll be there for the beginning, and the end."

-Forevermore
